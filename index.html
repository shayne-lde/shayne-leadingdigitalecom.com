The Real Story

Who I Actually Am

I grew up in Montreal. I wasn't the guy with a business degree or a trust fund or a clear plan. I was working as a valet driver — parking other people's cars — when the pandemic shut everything down in 2021.

That moment forced me to figure something out. I found Amazon dropshipping, specifically the FBM model, and went all in. There was no roadmap. Just a lot of trial and error, long nights, and relentless testing.

Within two years I had crossed $1 million in sales. Four years in, that number is over $8 million. I'm engaged to my partner of 11+ years — she's been there through all of it. We're building something real, and I'm grateful for every chapter of how we got here.

The Business

Leading Digital Ecom

Leading Digital Ecom is the coaching program I built around what actually worked for me on Amazon. Not theory. Not recycled YouTube content. The exact FBM dropshipping system I used to go from zero to $8M+.

We teach 1-on-1. My team and I work directly with each student — product research, listing strategy, pricing, scaling, automation. All of it, with real coaches who pick up the phone.

The program works for busy people — parents, 9-to-5 workers, people who only have 7–14 hours a week. That's who we built it for.
